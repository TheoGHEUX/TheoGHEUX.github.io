The Characters

Zaun

1 / 3

JINX

Criminally insane, lover of chaos and destruction, Jinx wreaks havoc in Piltover using her arsenal of explosive weapons, spreading panic wherever she steps.

2 / 3

VI

Vi, born Violet, is a Zaunite enforcer and the older sister of Jinx. Impulsive and brave, she excels in hand-to-hand combat. She wears a pair of powerful Hextech gauntlets that grant her immense strength.

3 / 3

EKKO

Ekko is a Zaunite inventor and the leader of the Firelights. He is also the childhood friend of Vi and Jinx. Hardened by the harsh tragedies he endured as a child, Ekko aspires to free the underground city from Silco and is willing to risk everything, including his own life, to achieve it.

Piltover

1 / 3

JAYCE

Jayce is a scientist and innovator who, after years of success, led the city's scientific efforts. As a result, he became the head of the Piltover council.

2 / 3

CAITLYN

Caitlyn Kiramman is a descendant of the Kiramman clan, one of the ruling houses of Piltover. She joined the Enforcers at a young age to make a name for herself. When her efforts to investigate a conspiracy in the underground city are blocked, Caitlyn sets out alone in search of answers, but she finds much more than she expected.

3 / 3

MEL

Mel Medarda is a Noxian-born member of the Piltover Council and an exiled member of the Medarda clan. Intelligent, ambitious, and a talented artist, she is a key figure in the Hextech project due to her close relationship with the lead scientist, Jayce Talis.
