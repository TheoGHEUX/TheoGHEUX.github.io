Fortiche Production is a French animation studio founded in 2009, known for its work on visually impressive projects. It is particularly famous for its collaboration with Riot Games on the animated series Arcane, based on League of Legends. The studio stands out for its ability to merge 2D and 3D animation, creating works with a unique and cinematic style. Fortiche is also involved in advertisements, music videos, and other ambitious projects, solidifying its international reputation in the animation industry.
